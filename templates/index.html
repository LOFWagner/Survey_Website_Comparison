Email Trustworthiness Survey

Welcome!

Please review the consent information below. By clicking "I Agree," you confirm that you consent to your fully anonymous responses being used for analysis in my bachelor's thesis.

This study aims to understand how people evaluate the trustworthiness of emails. Your participation will help me develop better strategies for email security awareness.

During this study, you will be asked to:

  • Provide basic demographic information
  • Review pairs of emails and select which ones you find more trustworthy
  • Optionally provide explanations for your choices

Your participation is voluntary, and responses are completely anonymous, no personally identifying information is saved.
