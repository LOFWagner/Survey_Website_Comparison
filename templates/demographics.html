Technical Proficiency Levels

Beginner: You have little to no experience with technology or programming. You may be unfamiliar with technical terms and require step-by-step guidance to complete tasks.

Intermediate: You have some experience with technology or programming. You can perform common tasks, understand basic concepts, and troubleshoot minor issues independently.

Advanced: You have extensive experience with technology or programming. You can solve complex problems, understand advanced concepts, and work independently on technical projects.
